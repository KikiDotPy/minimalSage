Hi, my name is Chiara, and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My passion started a long ago when there were still 56k modems and confusing weird colored websites.

I've started in my teens tinkering with computers: first by jumping into the magical world of videogames and PC hardware; and, later on, by discovering The Internet, where I started participating in building and organizing "places" that would allow people with the same passions and interest to be together.

I'm still passionate about tech and its power to help us build a future where everyone matters.

That's why I design and develop websites that are light, essential, and fast; that people are happy to use and that help people solve their problems and achieve their goals.
